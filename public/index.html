About

I'm a final year BSc Software Engineering student who builds things to understand them. My background is in software engineering and mathematics, and that combination has never felt coincidental. Maths gave me a way of thinking about problems that goes deeper than tutorials and frameworks. It's what eventually pulled me toward AI and machine learning, not as a buzzword but as an engineering discipline I wanted to actually understand.

Specifically, I'm interested in natural language processing and language models. How they learn structure from text, how retrieval augmented generation improves accuracy on real documents, how to design pipelines that hold up outside of demos. That's where I'm putting my learning right now, on top of the software engineering foundation I already have.

As a developer, I build full systems: backends, databases, APIs, cloud infrastructure. Everything in the projects section below is something I designed and built from scratch. No tutorials followed step by step. I pick a problem, figure out what it needs, and build it until it works.
